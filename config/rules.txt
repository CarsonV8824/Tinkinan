CATAN GAME RULES

=== SETUP ===
- Each player places 2 settlements and 2 roads on the board
- Settlements must be placed at least 2 intersections apart
- Each player receives resources from their second settlement

=== VICTORY CONDITION ===
- First player to reach 10 victory points wins

=== TURN STRUCTURE ===
1. Roll two dice
2. Resource production (all players with settlements/cities adjacent to tiles matching the roll collect resources)
3. Trading (optional)
4. Building (optional)

=== RESOURCES ===
- Brick (from hills)
- Lumber/Wood (from forest)
- Wool/Sheep (from pasture)
- Grain/Wheat (from fields)
- Ore (from mountains)

=== BUILDING COSTS ===
Road: 1 brick + 1 lumber
Settlement: 1 brick + 1 lumber + 1 wool + 1 grain
City: 3 ore + 2 grain
Development Card: 1 ore + 1 wool + 1 grain

=== VICTORY POINTS ===
- Settlement: 1 point
- City: 2 points
- Longest Road (5+ roads): 2 points
- Largest Army (3+ knights): 2 points
- Victory Point cards: 1 point each

=== THE ROBBER ===
- Activated when a 7 is rolled
- Players with 8+ cards must discard half at random (rounded down and slight change to rules)
- Active player moves robber to a new tile
- Robber blocks resource production on that tile
- Active player steals 1 random resource card from a player with a settlement/city on that tile

=== TRADING ===
- Trade with other players at any ratio (negotiable)
- Trade with bank at 4:1 ratio (4 identical resources for 1 of any type)
- Trade with ports at 3:1 or 2:1 ratios (if you have a settlement/city on a port)

=== DEVELOPMENT CARDS ===
- Knight: Move the robber and steal a resource
- Victory Point: +1 victory point (keep hidden until winning)
- Road Building: Place 2 free roads
- Year of Plenty: Take any 2 resources from the bank
- Monopoly: All players give you all cards of one declared resource type

=== SPECIAL RULES ===
- Cannot play a development card on the turn you bought it (except Victory Points)
- Can only play one development card per turn (Knights don't count toward this limit in some versions)
- Longest road must be continuous and at least 5 roads
- If longest road is broken or tied, the card returns to the table
